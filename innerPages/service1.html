Short Circuit Analysis

A short circuit analysis determines the maximum fault current for each piece of electrical equipment within an electrical distribution system.

Several scenarios are modeled using specialized software to simulate different fault contribution sources where applicable (ex: utility source with motor load, utility source without motor load, stand-by generator load).

Using SKM System Analysis, a model of the customerâ€™s facility electrical distribution is created with the power riser or one-line diagram as a reference.

Site surveys may be needed to collect additional equipment information for a complete and accurate analysis.

A comprehensive report summarizing all calculated faults under worst case scenario will be generated and included in the final analysis.
