Providing solutions to improve the reliability and safety of your electrical distribution systems

We are a specialized electrical engineering consulting firm focused on providing power analysis and system studies for electrical distribution infrastructures.

Our services are designed to support clients in optimizing the performance, safety, and reliability of their electrical systems as required by NFPA 70E and OSHA standards.

With over 20 years of experience in the electrical engineering field, our dedicated team has successfully delivered solutions across all six New England states.

Whether you are planning a new installation, or improving the safety and reliability of an existing system, our firm is your trusted partner in finding a solution for your challange.
